About Me

I am...

Filip Jęczmyk and I'm passionate about games. From an early age, I played all kinds of titles. Over a year ago, I decided to take matters into my own hands and start creating them. My goal is to be part of a game that will be explored all over the world.



I have been programming for 3 years and it is my passion and everyday life. 2D and 3D graphics are also familiar to me. I have mastered the following languages very well: C#, Python, Jvascript, CSS and the HTML markup language.
